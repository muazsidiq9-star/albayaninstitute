Arabic Literature

Explore classical and modern Arabic literature, its beauty, and depth.

Register Now

This course introduces students to the rich world of Arabic literature, from classical poetry and prose to modern works, enhancing reading, comprehension, and literary appreciation.

Module 1: Classical Poetry

Study pre-Islamic and classical Arabic poetry, understanding meters, rhymes, and themes.

Module 2: Classical Prose

Explore historical Arabic prose works, speeches, and writings from influential scholars and writers.

Module 3: Modern Literature

Learn about modern Arabic novels, short stories, and contemporary literary trends.

Module 4: Literary Analysis

Apply analysis techniques to poetry and prose to understand style, themes, and rhetorical devices.

Module 5: Writing & Expression

Practice composing short literary pieces, inspired by classical and modern Arabic literature.
