Intermediate Arabic

Enhance your Arabic vocabulary, grammar, and conversational skills.

Register Now

This course is ideal for learners who have basic knowledge of Arabic and want to improve their fluency, understanding, and communication skills.

Module 1: Vocabulary Expansion

Learn thematic vocabulary related to everyday topics and social situations.

Module 2: Grammar Review & Application

Review essential grammar points and practice applying them in sentences.

Module 3: Listening Comprehension

Develop listening skills through short dialogues and audio exercises.

Module 4: Speaking Practice

Engage in guided conversations and role-play exercises to improve fluency.

Module 5: Reading & Writing

Read moderately complex texts and practice writing short paragraphs.
