Nahw (Grammar)

Deep dive into Arabic syntax and sentence structure for advanced understanding.

Register Now

This course focuses exclusively on Nahw (Arabic grammar), helping learners master rules, sentence structures, and advanced syntax with clarity.

Module 1: Nominal Sentences

Understanding structure, subject-predicate agreement, and usage of الخبر (predicate).

Module 2: Verbal Sentences

Explore الفعل (verbs), فاعل (subject), and the proper order and conjugation rules.

Module 3: Cases & Endings

Master إعراب (i‘rāb) of nouns, verbs, and adjectives in different sentence positions.

Module 4: Particles & Connectors

Learn about حروف الجر (prepositions), أدوات الربط (connectors), and conditional phrases.

Module 5: Complex Sentences

Combine nominal and verbal sentences, subordinate clauses, and advanced syntactic structures.
