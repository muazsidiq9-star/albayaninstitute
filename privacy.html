Privacy Policy

At Al-Bayan Arabic Institute, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This page explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website or enroll in our programs.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information during registration, enrollment, or payment verification:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Country or location
  • Payment details submitted by students
  • Uploaded payment receipts
  • Program selection and learning preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for educational and administrative purposes.

  • To verify and confirm enrollment
  • To process payment confirmations
  • To communicate important class updates
  • To provide student support and guidance
  • To improve learning experience and services

3. Payment Processing

Some payments may be processed through trusted third-party payment platforms. Al-Bayan Arabic Institute does not store your debit or credit card information.

Payment receipts submitted by students are used strictly for payment verification purposes.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect student information against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

However, while we strive to protect your information, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted external services to support our platform and payment operations.

  • Selar (payment processing)
  • Supabase (database/storage)
  • EmailJS (email communication)

6. Student Responsibility

Students are responsible for ensuring that the information submitted during registration and payment confirmation is accurate.
