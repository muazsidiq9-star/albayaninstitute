Arabic Poetry

Learn the beauty of Arabic poetry, its rhythm, meaning, and expression.

Register Now

This course explores Arabic poetry from classical to modern times, teaching students to read, interpret, and appreciate poetic forms and expressions.

Module 1: Classical Arabic Poetry

Study pre-Islamic and classical Arabic poetry, meters, rhyme schemes, and themes.

Module 2: Modern Arabic Poetry

Explore contemporary Arabic poetry and its evolution in style and themes.

Module 3: Poetic Devices

Learn about metaphors, similes, symbolism, and other literary devices in Arabic poetry.

Module 4: Interpretation & Analysis

Practice interpreting poetry to understand meaning, context, and emotion.

Module 5: Composition & Recitation

Develop skills to compose short poems and recite with proper rhythm and expression.
