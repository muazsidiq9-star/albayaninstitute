At-Tawheed

Learn the fundamentals of Islamic monotheism and strengthen your belief in Allah’s oneness.

Register Now

This course is designed to provide a complete understanding of Tawheed, exploring its types, pillars, and practical implementation in daily life.

Module 1: Introduction to Tawheed

Understand the concept of monotheism and its importance in Islam.

Module 2: Tawheed al-Rububiyyah

Learn about Allah’s Lordship, His creation, sustenance, and governance of the universe.

Module 3: Tawheed al-Uluhiyyah

Focus on worshipping Allah alone, avoiding shirk, and understanding proper devotion.

Module 4: Tawheed al-Asma wa-Sifat

Explore Allah’s names and attributes and how to affirm them correctly without distortion.

Module 5: Practical Tawheed

Implement Tawheed in everyday life, avoiding innovation, and strengthening faith.
