Conversational Arabic

Focus on real-life speaking skills and everyday conversations in Arabic.

Register Now

This course helps learners build confidence in speaking Arabic for daily situations, greetings, shopping, and travel conversations.

Module 1: Greetings & Introductions

Learn how to greet people, introduce yourself, and ask basic questions in Arabic.

Module 2: Everyday Conversations

Practice talking about family, hobbies, and daily routines in Arabic.

Module 3: Shopping & Food

Learn how to ask prices, order food, and communicate in markets or restaurants.

Module 4: Travel & Directions

Handle transportation, ask for directions, and navigate travel situations confidently.

Module 5: Social Expressions

Understand common expressions, polite phrases, and cultural etiquette in conversations.
