Al-Fiqh

Learn Islamic jurisprudence from the basics to practical rulings for daily life.

Register Now

This course guides students through the foundational principles of Islamic law, gradually moving to advanced fiqh rulings for personal, social, and spiritual life.

Module 1: Introduction to Fiqh

Understanding what fiqh is, its sources, and its significance in guiding a Muslim’s life.

Module 2: Basic Worship Rulings

Learn the fiqh of prayer, fasting, zakat, and other core ibadah practices.

Module 3: Transactions & Ethics

Study halal/haram transactions, business ethics, and everyday dealings.

Module 4: Personal Status Laws

Explore marriage, divorce, inheritance, and family-related rulings.

Module 5: Contemporary Issues

Discuss modern-day challenges and how classical fiqh principles are applied today.
