Tafseerul Qur'an

Understand the meanings, wisdom, and lessons of the Qur’an in depth.

Register Now

This course takes students on a journey through the Qur’an, exploring the meanings of its verses, context of revelation, and timeless guidance for life.

Module 1: Introduction to Tafseer

Learn what Tafseer is, its importance, and the key principles for understanding the Qur’an correctly.

Module 2: Asbab al-Nuzul

Study the circumstances of revelation to grasp the context behind each verse.

Module 3: Tafseer by Meaning

Analyze selected Surahs and verses to extract guidance, morals, and practical lessons.

Module 4: Themes & Wisdom

Discover recurring themes, wisdom, and life lessons across the Qur’an.

Module 5: Application in Daily Life

Learn how to apply Qur’anic teachings in personal, social, and spiritual life.
