Tajweedul Qur'an

Master the rules of Qur’anic recitation and improve pronunciation.

Register Now

This course is designed for learners who want to perfect their Qur’anic recitation, understand Tajweed rules, and recite fluently with proper pronunciation.

Module 1: Introduction to Tajweed

Learn the basics of Tajweed, its importance, and how it affects Qur’anic recitation.

Module 2: Makharij & Sifaat

Understand articulation points (makharij) and characteristics (sifaat) of Arabic letters.

Module 3: Rules of Noon & Meem

Learn the proper application of Noon Saakinah and Meem Saakinah rules in recitation.

Module 4: Qalqalah & Madd

Practice Qalqalah letters and various types of Madd (elongation) in the Qur’an.

Module 5: Recitation Practice

Guided recitation exercises with feedback to improve fluency and accuracy.
