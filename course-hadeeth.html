Al-Hadeeth

Study the sayings and teachings of the Prophet ﷺ to gain guidance in belief and practice.

Register Now

This course introduces students to the science of Hadeeth, covering major collections, classification, and methodology for authentic narration.

Module 1: Introduction to Hadeeth

Learn what Hadeeth is and its role in Islam alongside the Qur’an.

Module 2: Types of Hadeeth

Explore the different categories such as Sahih, Hasan, Da’if, and others.

Module 3: Major Collections

Study key Hadeeth collections including Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Sunan Abu Dawood, and others.

Module 4: Hadeeth Authentication

Learn how scholars assess the chain of narrators (Isnad) and text (Matn) to determine authenticity.

Module 5: Practical Application

Understand how to implement the Prophet’s teachings in daily life, worship, and ethics.
